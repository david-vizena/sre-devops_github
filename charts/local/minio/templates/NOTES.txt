1. Retrieve credentials:
   ACCESS_KEY=$(kubectl get secret --namespace {{ .Release.Namespace }} {{ include "minio.fullname" . }} -o jsonpath="{.data.accesskey}" | base64 -d)
   SECRET_KEY=$(kubectl get secret --namespace {{ .Release.Namespace }} {{ include "minio.fullname" . }} -o jsonpath="{.data.secretkey}" | base64 -d)

2. Port-forward to access MinIO console locally:
   kubectl port-forward --namespace {{ .Release.Namespace }} svc/{{ include "minio.fullname" . }} 9000:{{ .Values.service.apiPort }} 9001:{{ .Values.service.consolePort }}

3. Open http://localhost:9001 and log in with the credentials above.
